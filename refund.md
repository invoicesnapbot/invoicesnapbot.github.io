## Free Tier

InvoiceSnap offers a free tier with 15 invoices per month. No payment is required, so no refund applies. If you're unsure whether InvoiceSnap is right for you, we encourage you to use the free tier first.

## Paid Tier: No Refunds After Service Begins

The paid tier ($9 USD/month for unlimited invoices) is a subscription-based digital service that begins immediately upon payment. Once your payment is processed and your account is upgraded, the service is considered active and no refunds are provided for that billing period.

### Why No Refunds?

Your $9 USD monthly subscription covers:

- Unlimited AI processing of your invoice data
- Continuous access to the extraction service
- Personality-driven commentary on your receipts

Because the service starts instantly and cannot be "returned," we do not offer refunds once the service has been delivered.

## Before You Pay

We encourage you to:

- Use the free tier (15 invoices/month) to test the service before upgrading
- Review the bot's capabilities by typing `!help` in Discord
- Ensure you have a valid Google account that can accept editor invitations
- Understand that AI extraction may not be 100% accurate
- Contact us at admin@invoicesnapbot.com if you have questions before subscribing

## Exception: Technical Failure

If a technical failure on our end prevents the service from functioning after successful payment, we will:

- Attempt to resolve the issue promptly
- If unresolved within 48 hours (excluding circumstances beyond our control, such as power outages, natural disasters, or third-party service outages), provide a full refund

This does not apply to:

- User error (incorrect Google account, inability to accept editor invitations, etc.)
- Dissatisfaction with extraction accuracy
- Change of mind after upgrading
- Issues caused by Discord, Google, or Stripe outages beyond our control

## How to Request a Refund (Technical Failures Only)

Email admin@invoicesnapbot.com with:

- Your Discord username
- The email address associated with your Stripe payment
- Date and approximate time of payment
- Description of the issue

We aim to respond within 3 business days.

## Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us violates our Terms of Service and may result in permanent suspension from using InvoiceSnap.

## Questions?

Email admin@invoicesnapbot.com before upgrading if you're unsure about anything.
