# InvoiceSnap Refund Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

**Service:** InvoiceSnap Discord bot with Google Sheets integration  
**Location:** Australia  
**Contact:** admin@invoicesnapbot.com  

---

## Our Policy: No Refunds After Service Begins

InvoiceSnap provides a **subscription‑based digital service that begins immediately**. Once your Google Sheet is created and you have access, the service is considered active and **no refunds are provided**.

### Why No Refunds?

Your $9 monthly subscription covers:
- Immediate creation of your personalised Google Sheet
- Ongoing AI processing of your invoice data
- Continuous access to the extraction service

Because the service starts instantly and cannot be "returned," we do not offer refunds once the service has been delivered.

## Before You Pay

We encourage you to:
- Review the bot's capabilities by typing `!help` in Discord
- Ensure you have a valid Google account that can accept editor invitations
- Understand that AI extraction may not be 100% accurate
- Contact us at **admin@invoicesnapbot.com** if you have questions before subscribing

## Exception: Technical Failure

If a **technical failure on our end** prevents the Google Sheet from being created after successful payment, we will:
1. Attempt to resolve the issue promptly
2. If unresolved within 48 hours, provide a full refund

This does **not** apply to:
- User error (incorrect Google account, inability to accept editor invitations, etc.)
- Dissatisfaction with extraction accuracy
- Change of mind after receiving the sheet
- Ability to use `!setup` with a different email address (you can change the linked email at any time, though previously extracted data will not be transferred)

## How to Request a Refund (Technical Failures Only)

Email **admin@invoicesnapbot.com** with:
- Your Discord username
- Date and approximate time of payment
- Description of the issue

We aim to respond within 3 business days.

## Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us violates our Terms of Service and may result in permanent suspension from using InvoiceSnap.

## Questions?

Email **admin@invoicesnapbot.com** before subscribing if you're unsure about anything.
